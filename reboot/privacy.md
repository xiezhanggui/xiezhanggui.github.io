# Reboot Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Reboot app ("Reboot", "we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. Reboot is built to be private by default.

## 1. No Account, No Data Collection
Reboot does **not** require an account, and we do **not** collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server we own. Everything you create in the app — your streak, start date, pledge, onboarding answers, reflections, and progress — is stored **only on your device**.

## 2. No Tracking, No Ads
Reboot contains no advertising and no third-party tracking or analytics SDKs. We do not build a profile of you and we do not sell or share your information, because we never receive it.

## 3. Subscriptions
Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive your payment details. Apple may provide us with anonymized, aggregated information about subscription status so the app can unlock Pro features. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

## 4. Notifications
Notifications are optional and off by default. If you enable reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. We do not send push notifications from a server.

## 5. Data Deletion
Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in control. Using "Clear local cache" in the app, or deleting the app, permanently removes your data. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete.

## 6. Children
Reboot is intended for adults (rated 17+) and is not directed to children.

## 7. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

# Reboot 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 22 日

本隐私政策说明 Reboot App（"Reboot"、"我们"或"本 App"）如何处理你的信息。Reboot 默认即注重隐私。

## 1. 无账号，不收集数据
Reboot **无需**账号，我们**不会**在任何由我们拥有的服务器上收集、传输或存储你的个人数据。你在 App 内产生的一切——连续天数、起始日期、誓言、引导测评答案、反思与进度——都**仅存储在你的设备上**。

## 2. 无追踪，无广告
Reboot 不含任何广告，也不含任何第三方追踪或分析 SDK。我们不会为你建立画像，也不会出售或分享你的信息——因为我们根本不会拿到这些信息。

## 3. 订阅
订阅购买完全由 Apple 处理。我们不会获取你的支付信息。Apple 可能向我们提供匿名化、聚合的订阅状态信息，以便 App 解锁 Pro 功能。这受 Apple 隐私政策约束。

## 4. 通知
通知为可选项，且默认关闭。若你开启提醒，提醒将在你的设备本地排程。我们不会从服务器推送通知。

## 5. 数据删除
由于你的数据仅存在于你的设备上，主动权始终在你手中。在 App 内使用"清除本地缓存"，或删除本 App，即可永久移除你的数据。我们的服务器上没有任何可删除的内容。

## 6. 儿童
Reboot 面向成年人（分级 17+），不面向儿童。

## 7. 政策变更
我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。变更生效后继续使用本 App 即表示接受修订后的政策。
