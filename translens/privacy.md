# Privacy Policy

**TransLens**

_Last updated: May 30, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how TransLens ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we process.

By using TransLens, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Process

### 1.1 Content You Provide for Translation
When you translate text, voice, or photos, the content you submit is processed to produce a translation.

- **On-device translation:** When the app uses Apple's on-device translation engine, your content is processed entirely on your device and is **not** sent to any server.
- **Cloud translation:** When on-device translation is unavailable for a language pair, the text is sent over an encrypted connection to third-party translation providers (see Section 3) solely to return a translation. We do not use this content to identify you, and it is not stored on our own servers.

Camera images are processed on your device for text recognition (OCR). Photos are **not** uploaded to our servers.

### 1.2 Device and Usage Information
We and our service providers may process:

- **Device identifiers** (including the Advertising Identifier / IDFA) — used to deliver and measure advertising.
- **Advertising data** — interactions with ads.
- **Product interaction data** — feature usage and in-app events, used for analytics to improve the app.
- **Crash and diagnostic data** — to detect, diagnose, and fix problems.
- **Approximate region / device locale** — to provide region-appropriate results and language defaults.

### 1.3 Information We Do **Not** Collect
- We do not require you to create an account.
- We do not ask for your name, email, contacts, or precise location.
- We do not sell your personal data.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- Provide translation features (text, voice, and camera).
- Display and measure advertising within the app.
- Understand how the app is used and improve its features and stability.
- Detect and fix crashes and technical problems.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

TransLens relies on the following third-party services, each of which processes data under its own privacy policy:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | Advertising | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics) | Analytics and crash reporting | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |
| Google AI (Gemini) | Cloud translation (when used) | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| MyMemory | Cloud translation fallback | https://mymemory.translated.net/doc/privacy.php |
| Apple Translation | On-device translation | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |

When cloud translation is used, only the text needed to perform the translation is transmitted.

---

## 4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, the app will ask for your permission before accessing your device's Advertising Identifier for the purpose of personalized advertising.

- If you **allow** tracking, you may see more relevant ads.
- If you **do not allow** tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized using your Advertising Identifier.

You can change this choice at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking** on your device.

---

## 5. Advertising

We display advertising provided by Google AdMob. Advertising partners may use device identifiers and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. Where required, a consent prompt (for example, under GDPR via Google's User Messaging Platform) is presented before personalized ads are shown.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Content submitted for cloud translation is processed transiently to return a result and is not retained on our servers.
- Analytics, advertising, and crash data are retained by the respective third-party providers according to their own retention policies.

---

## 7. Your Choices

- **Tracking:** Manage in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
- **Personalized ads:** Manage via your device's advertising settings and, where applicable, the in-app consent options.
- **Translation history / saved items:** Stored locally on your device; you can delete them at any time within the app, and they are removed when you delete the app.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

TransLens is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## 9. International Users

TransLens is available globally. By using the app, you understand that your information may be processed by our third-party service providers in countries other than your own, under the protections described in their respective privacy policies.

---

## 10. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted network connections, to protect information in transit. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

# 隱私政策

**TransLens**

_最後更新：2026 年 5 月 30 日_

本隱私政策說明 TransLens（「我們」或「本 App」）在你使用我們的 iOS 應用程式時如何處理資訊。我們致力於保護你的隱私，並對所處理的資料保持透明。

使用 TransLens 即表示你同意本政策所述的做法。

---

## 1. 我們處理的資訊

### 1.1 你提供以進行翻譯的內容
當你翻譯文字、語音或照片時，你提交的內容會被處理以產生翻譯結果。

- **裝置端翻譯：** 當 App 使用 Apple 的裝置端翻譯引擎時，你的內容完全在裝置上處理，**不會**傳送到任何伺服器。
- **雲端翻譯：** 當某語言對無法使用裝置端翻譯時，文字會透過加密連線傳送給第三方翻譯服務（見第 3 節），僅用於回傳翻譯結果。我們不會用這些內容辨識你的身分，也不會儲存在我們自己的伺服器上。

相機影像會在你的裝置上進行文字辨識（OCR）。照片**不會**上傳到我們的伺服器。

### 1.2 裝置與使用資訊
我們及我們的服務供應商可能會處理：

- **裝置識別碼**（包含廣告識別碼 / IDFA）— 用於投放與衡量廣告。
- **廣告資料** — 與廣告的互動。
- **產品互動資料** — 功能使用與 App 內事件，用於分析以改善 App。
- **當機與診斷資料** — 用於偵測、診斷及修復問題。
- **概略地區 / 裝置語系** — 用於提供符合地區的結果與預設語言。

### 1.3 我們**不會**收集的資訊
- 我們不要求你建立帳號。
- 我們不會索取你的姓名、電子郵件、通訊錄或精確位置。
- 我們不會出售你的個人資料。

---

## 2. 我們如何使用資訊

我們使用上述資訊以：

- 提供翻譯功能（文字、語音與相機）。
- 在 App 內顯示與衡量廣告。
- 了解 App 的使用情形，並改善其功能與穩定性。
- 偵測並修復當機及技術問題。

---

## 3. 第三方服務

TransLens 依賴下列第三方服務，各服務皆依其各自的隱私政策處理資料：

| 服務 | 用途 | 隱私政策 |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | 廣告 | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Firebase（Analytics 與 Crashlytics） | 分析與當機回報 | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |
| Google AI（Gemini） | 雲端翻譯（使用時） | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| MyMemory | 雲端翻譯備援 | https://mymemory.translated.net/doc/privacy.php |
| Apple Translation | 裝置端翻譯 | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |

使用雲端翻譯時，僅傳送執行翻譯所需的文字。

---

## 4. App 追蹤透明度（ATT）

依據 Apple 的 App 追蹤透明度框架，本 App 會在為了個人化廣告而存取你裝置的廣告識別碼前，先徵求你的許可。

- 若你**允許**追蹤，你可能會看到更相關的廣告。
- 若你**不允許**追蹤，你仍會看到廣告，但不會以你的廣告識別碼進行個人化。

你可隨時在裝置的**設定 → 隱私權與安全性 → 追蹤**中變更此選擇。

---

## 5. 廣告

我們透過 Google AdMob 顯示廣告。廣告合作夥伴可能使用裝置識別碼與廣告互動資料來投放與衡量廣告。在法規要求時（例如依 GDPR 透過 Google 的使用者訊息平台），會在顯示個人化廣告前出現同意提示。

---

## 6. 資料保留

- 提交至雲端翻譯的內容僅暫時處理以回傳結果，不會保留於我們的伺服器。
- 分析、廣告與當機資料由各第三方供應商依其自身的保留政策保留。

---

## 7. 你的選擇

- **追蹤：** 於 iOS 設定 → 隱私權與安全性 → 追蹤 中管理。
- **個人化廣告：** 透過裝置的廣告設定，以及（適用時）App 內的同意選項管理。
- **翻譯紀錄 / 已儲存項目：** 儲存於你的裝置本機；你可隨時在 App 內刪除，刪除 App 時亦會一併移除。
