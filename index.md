## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I get help with a specific app?**
Email us at the address above and mention the app's name. We'll get back to you as soon as we can.

**How do I request a feature or report a bug?**
Send us an email with the details. We genuinely consider every suggestion.

**Where can I find an app's privacy policy?**
Each app has its own privacy policy, linked in the list below.

**How do I manage ad and tracking preferences?**
You can change tracking permission anytime in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking** on your device.
